The Band

Something Blue started in a basement in 2021 — four friends, a borrowed reverb pedal, and a stubborn belief that quiet songs can hit hardest.

Two records and a hundred-odd shows later, the band trades in widescreen guitars, patient drums, and choruses that arrive like weather. Midnight Tide is their most expansive work yet — recorded live to tape over one cold winter on the coast.
